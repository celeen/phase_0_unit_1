Why is pair programming important?

  1. Good practice at Collaboration
  2. Good opportunity to learn
  3. It works best if your stuck with a problem
  4. Great for debugging!

Pairing is programming in pairs working together on a problem simulataneously looking for a solution.

Driver and Navigator

Driver is the one who has his/her hands on keyboard or the one who is putting down the code, whereas Navigator is directing the driver for the code.
